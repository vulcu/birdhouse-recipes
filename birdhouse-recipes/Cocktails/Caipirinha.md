# Caipirinha

## Ingredients

| Qty.     | Item            |
| -------- | --------------- |
| 2 1/2 oz | Cacha√ßa         |
| 1/2      | Lime, quartered |
| 1-2 tsp  | Sugar           |

## Steps

1. In a large rocks glass, squeeze and drop in 2 eighths of lime.
1. Add sugar, muddle (crush) and mix with a spoon.
1. Pour in the cachaca and plenty of ice. Stir well.
1. Garnish with lime wheel.
